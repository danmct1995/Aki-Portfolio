Board Game Concept And Design

This was a project I did at Westfield State University. The only guidelines for this project were to design a boardgame, outside of that, I had complete creative freedom. I decided to create a choice-based questionaire game, where the better a choice may be for the environment, the more spaces forward the player will move.

The concept, branding, and illustrations are all done by me.
