My First Branding Project

This was a very special project to me, that gave me the confidence to start freelancing. For this project, I was given an animal and an industry to create a brand. I was assigned drinks as an industry and bee's as my animal. Under these conditions, I decided to create a preworkout brand called "enerbee", since fitness has a big role in my life.

This project involved designing a logo, brand style guide, icons, ads, posters, website, process book, and more. Visit Here to view the entire process book on Issuu.
