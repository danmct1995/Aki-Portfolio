Infographic Design for Rhythm

This is a project I worked on for Rhythm, a renewable energy provider based in Texas. The client was looking to create several infographics to be used on their website to help their customer's understanding of how the company works. They provided me with the copy, inspiration images, illustrator files of existing illustrations, and a rough layout of the final infographics. I designed the infographics by following their brand style guides and creating custom images where needed.
