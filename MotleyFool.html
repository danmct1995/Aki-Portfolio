Social Media Infographics for the Motley Fool

These are some of the infographics I created for the Motley fool. I was either given an article to pull important points from or the copy was provided to me. Outside of that, I was given complete creative freedom in terms of graphical elements, colors, and font choices. In some cases, I was asked to create sveral different aspect ratios of the same design so that they could be used in multiple social media platforms.
