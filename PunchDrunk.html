Website Illustrations for a Video Production Company

I was hired to create several illustrations and icons to be used throughout the website. The client provided me with a few illustration styles that he liked, and I chose the one that went with the feel of the brand and adjusted it to make it into a unique style. I started out by designing 6 characters that were going to be appearing throughout the illustrations.

Starting Point & Reference for the Intended Style

"Cyborg" from Icons80 - bright, techy, floating (not pinned to a background), selective color palette
