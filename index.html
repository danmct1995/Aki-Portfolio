Hi, I do general graphic design, brand style for startup companies, and vector illustrations!

I'm Aki, I moved to the United States when I was ten years old, and ever since then, art and creating has been my bestfriend. Art classes were there to comfort me when I was struggling with the language barrier and teaching my fellow classmates how to make origami was how I first made friends.

Since then, my passion for creating has continued to develop and I now produce vector illustrations and branding for my clients as a freelancer!
