Branding for Wordpress Quiz Plugin Company

For this project, I was asked to create a branding for a quiz plugin company. They had a logo designed that they liked, but it looked too similar to BuzzFeed's quiz logo, so they wanted to differentiate themselves.

Client Review

"Great attention to details and time. I have always received work on the mentioned time and not a minute later which is extremely important. The designs received exceeded expectations and I am honestly amazed by the quality of work received."

The client Stressed that he really liked the original logo, so in order to honor that, I kept the style of the text exactly the same and added shapes within the text to differentiate and improve on their design. The client also wanted a logo option, where an icon was added to their logo.
